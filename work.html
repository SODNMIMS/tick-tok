All the prototypes

Prototyping is the conversation you have with your ideas. Design and prototyping go hand in hand. If one of them is eliminated, the fimal design is effected. Prototyping enables quick and inexpensive iterations for user testing to create modifications & mould the design according to the user.

The team has used elements like arduino boards, bread boards, RTC boards, EPS Boards, Thingspeak, p5js, ml5, etc. to create the prototypes.

From creation

The form of this clock was inspired from the quote “time is a target to meet”. The form of a target was referred to while making this clock. The rings of the clock suggest the rings of the target and the led lights showing the time suggest how target and time never remain constant but keep moving forward.
