Diving deeper into System Thinking

It is important to look at the whole system

What did we do?

The face of a user has to be captured while entering through the door.

Execution

We used a OV7670 Camera in order to capture the face of a person.

We connected the camera with the Arduino using a break board.

The intention was to capture the face and push it on the cloud for storing the data of regular late commers.

However, the working of the camera was uncessful.

Pivot Steps

To use a ESP Board and connect it to a webcam.

To push the data to ThingSpeak.

To incorporate machine learning using p5js and ml5 to identify students in order to maintain the record.
