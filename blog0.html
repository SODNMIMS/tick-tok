What did we do?

Demonstrate that the buzzing of the door when the user comes late (Part 1)

Execution

We initially used a Peezo buzzer to demonstrate the buzzer mechanism of the door.

The Peezo buzzer was conected with an Arduino board and the circuit was powered up.

It was azzumed that the Peezo buzzer will buz if there is an interference in the path of the infrared ray it reflects.

The Peezo buzzer requires a stability of the environment surrounding it in order to identify disrupancies in future.

However, due to the lack of environmental stability in the environment of installment, the use of Peezo Buzzer was eleminated.

Pivot Steps

To use a Magnetic Reed Switch instead of a Peezo buzzer for accurate results.
